Having texture loss / city bug?

Details on how to fix this are explained below.

Is your game looking like this?

Don't worry your pc (usually) isn't the problem. Read below to see how to fix this issue!

Go to Settings > Graphics, and scroll to "Extended Texture Budget"

Turn this up, Keep an eye on your Video Memory, don't let this bar go red.

If you are running a lower-end pc, you might need to turn down your texture quality

Voila
